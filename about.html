Period-poverty is an non profitable organisation that is intended to assists young girls from under-priviledged background with sanitary pads. Period-poverty is lack of access to menstrual products, hygiene facilities, waste management. This organisation allows people to donate sanitary pads and anything that can contribute to assisting young girls. We are intending to deliver pads to schools,churches and most specifically orphanages. Our goal is to allow young girls to belive to express themeselves and have confidence .We want girls to never miss out on school due to period-poverty. With this organisation, we can be able to change the narrative that the society has of being silent about it and making it all about woman. Men should also educate themeselves about periods and familarise themselves so that young girls can also rely on them
